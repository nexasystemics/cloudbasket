CloudBasket Refund & Returns Policy Concerns
Date: 02 April 2026

1. The published refund policy now assumes an affiliate plus third-party marketplace POD model.
   If CloudBasket later activates direct checkout, payment capture, direct fulfilment, or direct
   order management, this policy will need immediate revision.

2. Current repository files still indicate direct POD commerce capability:
   - app/api/pod/checkout/create-order/route.ts
   - app/api/pod/checkout/verify-payment/route.ts
   - app/pod/page.tsx
   These files do not align cleanly with the new policy position.

3. Until the board decides on deletion or deactivation of POD checkout flows, there remains a
   product-to-policy contradiction risk if those flows become reachable or are interpreted as live.

4. If CloudBasket ever becomes merchant of record for any SKU, a separate direct-sale refund and
   cancellation regime will be required, including timelines, damaged-item handling, and customer
   support ownership.

5. The current policy uses info@cloudbasket.co as the issue-reporting contact. If the board wants
   a dedicated legal, grievance, or policy mailbox, the policy and PDF view should be updated.
