## 1. PROJECT SCOPE & VISION
CloudBasket is a premium, "Sovereign UI" price aggregator and affiliate discovery hub specifically engineered for the Indian and Global marketplaces. The platform prioritizes high-speed discovery, user privacy, and visual elegance (Apple-clean aesthetic).

### 🛡️ THE ZERO-CHECKOUT MANDATE (CORE PILLAR)
CloudBasket operates as a pure discovery engine. 
- **No Local Shopping Cart:** Users do not complete purchases within the app.
- **No Payment Processing:** No sensitive financial data (Stripe, etc.) is stored or handled.
- **Income Shield Node:** All "Buy" actions are securely routed through the `/go/[id]` redirection node to verified external affiliate partners (Amazon, Flipkart, CJ, etc.), ensuring maximum security and attribution tracking.
