- MAIN_CATEGORIES in lib/constants.ts contains values like:
  - Beauty & Care
  - Food & Grocery
  - Home & Kitchen
  - Laptops & PCs
  - Print on Demand
- SUB_CATEGORIES in lib/mock-data.ts is keyed by:
  - Beauty
  - Grocery
  - Home
  - Laptops
  - Toys
- Exact overlap between the two sets is only:
  - Automotive
  - Books
  - Fashion
  - Mobiles
  - Sports
- Result:
  - The current generateProducts() logic in lib/mock-data.ts is consistent only for those exact matches.
  - Estimated current generated mock total under current code shape is 1,000 items, not 2,000.

- lib/cloudbasket-data.ts contains the curated storefront catalog.
- lib/products-data.ts contains a separate 48-product normalized grid catalog.
- lib/mock-data.ts contains generated mock products for compare/search/admin style flows.
- lib/intelligence/catalog.ts contains another curated intelligence-oriented catalog.
